Short-term Rental Regulations Impact Analysis (2010-2024)

This application presents an analysis of Google search trends related to short-term rental regulations, housing affordability, and labor markets over a 15-year period.

Understanding the Data

The data used in this analysis represents Google search query volumes for various terms related to short-term rentals from 2010 to 2024. Here's what you need to know:

  • Data Source: Google Trends API, which provides search interest data on a scale of 0 to 100.
  • Interpretation: Higher values indicate greater search interest for a particular term relative to other time periods.
  • Limitations: This data reflects search interest, not actual economic or policy metrics. It can provide insights into public attention and concerns but should not be interpreted as direct measures of economic conditions or policy effectiveness.
  • Time Range: The data covers the period from 2010 to 2024, allowing us to observe long-term trends in public interest regarding these topics.
